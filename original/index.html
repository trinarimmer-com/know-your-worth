A Salary Negotiation Simulation — in two versions.

In 2016, I built a branching salary negotiation simulation in Rise using free stock photos, PowerPoint-animated video, and my own voice. It was lean, cheap, and effective. A decade later, I rebuilt it using AI tools to demonstrate that good instructional design doesn't require elaborate production — it requires clear thinking.

Both versions are here. Try them, compare them, and draw your own conclusions.
